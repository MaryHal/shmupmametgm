Fumen Encoder




Note: The generated fumen links can be very long if you encode level/game time and the server that hosts fumen can't handle URLs longer than ~8195 characters. To bypass this, a second link is generated as a bookmarklet so you can copy and paste it into the addressbar at fumen.zui.jp. The latest version of fumen also has a hard limit of 32768 characters on the length of string it can decode, so your fumen may still be cut short.
